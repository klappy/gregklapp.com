About the Book

In this powerful memoir, Gregory D. Klapp shares his journey from surviving a near-fatal stroke and heart bypass to discovering the healing power of a whole food, plant based lifestyle. Filled with personal stories, practical tips, and scientific insights, this book is a guide for anyone seeking to transform their health and embrace new beginnings.

About the Author

Gregory D. Klapp is an advocate for plant based nutrition who travels nationwide sharing his story and inspiring others to take control of their health. His message is one of hope, empowerment, and the belief that change is possible at any stage of life.
